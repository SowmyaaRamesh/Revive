Who are we?

We are a team of conscious individuals working towards reviving our planet one step at a time by funding a variety of environmental initiatives as a non-profit public benefit organisation. We are committed to raising public environmental consciousness, as well as actively implementing change. Our mission is to make lasting changes by raising the environmental awareness of all people. Please join us in spreading the word, "Revive our Planet". Time is running out.

Why you should care

Oh, why you ask? Let's look at a few facts, shall we?

Every minute, one garbage truck of plastic is dumped into our oceans. Rainforests are cut down at a rate of 100 acres per minute. According to NASA, the world's rainforests will be gone by 2100 if the current rate of destruction continues. The world population is 215,000 people larger today than it was yesterday. If current patterns continue, we will have emptied the world’s oceans for seafood by 2050. There will be no water by 2040 if we keep doing what we're doing today. By 2100, the global urban population will produce three times more waste than today. A glass bottle made in our time will take more than 4,000 years to decompose. An estimated 50,000 species inhabiting our tropical forests become extinct annually. That’s an average of 137 species a day.
